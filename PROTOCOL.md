# Ross Ice Shelf Benthic Life Exploration Protocol

## Sample Preservation

### For DNA Analysis

Tissue samples should be preserved in 95% ethanol for long-term storage. Use sterile forceps to place samples in cryovials filled with ethanol. Store at -20°C if possible, otherwise at 4°C. Label vials with unique sample IDs, date, location, and depth.
